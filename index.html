Barteree Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Send an email to bartereeapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your phone number associated with the account
  3. We will process your request within 7 business days

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your user profile
  • All your item listings
  • Your trading history
  • Your messages and communications
  • Your profile photo

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • Transaction records (for legal compliance)
  • Log data (for security purposes)

This data will be retained for a maximum of 90 days after account deletion.
